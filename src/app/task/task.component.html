About us

SALAHADDIN FUTURE STARS LANGUAGE SCHOOL, which was launched in 2011-2012 Academic Year respond to the expectations of many people in Beni Suewf, Egypt, is proud to achieve trust and success.


As being used the latest technology at SALAHALDIN for the visual support, all the classrooms in Kindergarten and Primary sections are equipped with projectors, sound system. Presentation rooms
equipped with projectors for Student-Teacher-Parent training and seminars.

Laboratories equipped with the latest technology to learn by experiments, using audio and visual drills. With the aim of achieving the best in artistic activities, music, art and home economic classes are designed in the best way.



















What We Do?

The mission of SFS is to equip students with the necessary tools and essential skills vital to achieving academic excellence,
